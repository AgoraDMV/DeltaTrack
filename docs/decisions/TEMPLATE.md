# N. Short title in the imperative (e.g. "Use X for Y")

- Status: Proposed
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints, and what makes the choice
non-obvious. State the facts a reader needs, including any evidence (numbers,
parity checks, links). Set the decision up; do not argue for it yet.

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly in the active voice ("We will ..."). Name the main
alternatives considered and why they lost, so the reasoning stays recoverable.

## Consequences

What becomes true after this decision: the benefits, the costs, the new
constraints, and any follow-up work or open risks. Capture the bad with the good.
